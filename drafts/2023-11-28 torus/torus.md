
$$
\begin{split}
&x(\theta,\varphi) = (R+r\cos\theta)\cos\varphi\\
&y(\theta,\varphi) = (R+r\cos\theta)\sin\varphi\\
&z(\theta,\varphi) = r\sin\theta\\
\end{split}
$$

$\theta,\varphi\in[0,2\pi]$
